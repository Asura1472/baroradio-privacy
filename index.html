Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Baro Radio ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not require account registration.

The App does not intentionally collect personally identifiable information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Home address
  • Government identification

Automatically Collected Information

Some information may be collected automatically by third-party services used by the App, including:

  • Device model
  • Android version
  • Crash logs
  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • Advertising identifiers (if applicable)

Advertising

The App may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google and its partners may use advertising identifiers to provide personalized or non-personalized ads in accordance with applicable laws and user consent.

Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

Permission Purpose
Internet Stream radio stations and load station information.
Network State Detect internet connectivity.
Foreground Service Continue radio playback while the app is in the background.
Notifications (Android 13+) Display playback controls.

Radio Streams

The App provides convenient access to publicly available internet radio streams. The App does not own, host, or modify the radio content. All copyrights and broadcasting rights belong to their respective owners.

Third-Party Services

The App may use the following services:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Firebase (Crash Reporting and Analytics, if enabled)
  • GitHub Pages (Station list updates)

Data Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect information processed by the App. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes become effective immediately after publication on this page.
